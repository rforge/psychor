posturo(isotone)R Documentation

Repeated posturographic measures

Description

This dataset represents a subset from the posturographic data collected in Leitner et al. (sensory organisation test SOT).

Usage

data(mendota)

Format

A data frame with 50 subjects, age as predictor and 3 repeated SOT measures as responses.

References

Leitner, C., Mair, P., Paul, B., Wick, F., Mittermaier, C., Sycha, T., & Ebenbichler, G. (2008). Reliability of posturographic measurements in the assessment of impaired sensorimotor function in chronic low back pain. Journal of Electromyography and Kinesiology, forthcoming.

Examples

data(posturo)
